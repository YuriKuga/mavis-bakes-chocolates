About us

Nestled in the heart of Lima, Perú, Mavis Bakes & Chocolates stands as a testament to a rich heritage steeped in the artistry of pastry and chocolate, founded over 3 years ago by Kaori, a visionary confectioner with a passion for excellence, the journey of Mavis Bakes & Chocolates began modestly.

From its humble origins, Mavis Bakes & Chocolates quickly gained renown for its commitment to using only the finest ingredients sourced locally and globally. Each creation bore the hallmark of artisanal craftsmanship, blending traditional techniques with innovative flair.

Over the years, Mavis expanded its repertoire, introducing a wide array of pastries and chocolates that reflected both classic elegance and contemporary tastes, every creation bore witness to the passion and expertise of its creators.
